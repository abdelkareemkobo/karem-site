Kareemai - Digital Garden 🌱

Hello, I’m Abdelkareem Elkhateb, an AI Engineer passionate about learning and developing new research ideas in LLMs, NLP, and information retrieval.

I am dedicated to applying this knowledge in real-world applications and advancing open-source Arabic AI projects.

I am one of the maintainers of the NAMAA community, which accelerates AI applications for Arabic and Islamic culture.

This blog documents my journey in programming, deep learning, and AGI.

Currently, I am focused on learning:

  1. Signal Processing: Building speech processing and AI applications for human voice understanding.

  2. Generative AI: Building generative models for business solutions and creative applications.

  3. Recommendation Systems: Passionate about personalization—enjoy designing recommendation systems for projects of all sizes.

  4. Information Retrieval: Working with embeddings and vector databases to improve search and knowledge discovery.

  5. Hypermedia Systems: Developing small apps with htmx to accelerate AI pipelines, from data processing to observability.
